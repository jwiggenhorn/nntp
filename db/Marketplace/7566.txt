Today we received record-shattering house price numbers. Although major metro 
housing market conditions right now are sharply reminiscent of the bubble of 
2005, economists consider this a very different category of boom. Also on 
today’s show, how Louisianans without power or water are surviving in the 
late-summer heat, a stark report on racial and ethnic disparity in receiving 
specialty health care and a look at the businesses — 6% of borrowers — that have
been asked to pay back portions of their Paycheck Protection Program loans.

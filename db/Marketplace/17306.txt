“There was a lot of gasping going on,” was how Heather Long of The Washington 
Post described the reception this morning to August’s disappointing, 
very-big-miss jobs report. Given that the delta variant is still very much at 
large, have economists and labor market analysts been too optimistic about job 
creation? Also on today’s show: the costly steps to securing a more resilient 
New York subway system and some background on why Apple is relaxing its app 
store rules.

Network security solutions provider Fortinet confirmed that a malicious actor 
had unauthorizedly disclosed VPN login names and passwords associated with 
87,000 FortiGate SSL-VPN devices. "These credentials were obtained from systems 
that remained unpatched against CVE-2018-13379 at the time of the actor's scan. 
While they may have since been patched, if the passwords were not reset, they
